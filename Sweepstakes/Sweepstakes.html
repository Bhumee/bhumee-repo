Oban Distillery ‐ one of the oldest and smallest distilleries
in Scotland - has been making fine Single Malt Scotch Whisky
since 1794. The distillery sits nestled on the west coast of
Scotland between granite cliffs and the sea in a picturesque
harbour resort town it helped name. Indeed, Oban the town
exists because of the distillery. The founders, the pioneering
brothers John and Hugh Stevenson, first established a brewery
and then a distillery on a piece of land right next to the bay
and the success of these ventures prompted the establishment
and growth of the town around it.
